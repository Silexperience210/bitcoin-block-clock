LIVE PREVIEW — RENDERED FROM THE FIRMWARE LAYOUT

Three steps, one cable

Flashing happens entirely in the browser over Web Serial. Nothing is installed on your computer and nothing leaves it.

STEP 01

Connect

Plug the clock into your computer with a USB-C data cable. No driver is needed on Windows 11, macOS or Linux.

STEP 02

Flash it

Press Flash it, pick the serial port (USB JTAG/serial), and keep the tab open while the firmware writes.

STEP 03

Boot

The board restarts on its own and opens a Wi-Fi setup portal (BlockClock-Setup) on first boot. Join it, enter your network, done.
